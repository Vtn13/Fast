Largest Bannana car

In 2009 Michigan resident Steve Braithwaite
started building the longestcustom banana car
to ever have been made.
Using the body of a 1993 Ford F150 Pickup truck
Steve started building his dream banana car.
After two long years he finally finished.
The car spanned 22 ft and 10.5 inches it was a pretty long banana.
The Car had a top speed of 136.79 km/ph it was fast for a berry.
This is one of the mostinteresting cars in the world.

Largest Loop De Loop

The Largest Loop De Loop was 19.43 meters or 63.11ft.
It was achieved 25th November 2019 in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia
by Englishman Terry Grant.
Terry Grant was also the previous record holder
for the largest loop de loop. He broke his own record.
