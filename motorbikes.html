Fastest Motorbike speed achieved

The fastest motorcycle speed ever achieved
is an average speed of 605.697 km/ph this was over 0.6 miles.
This record was achieved by Rocky Robinson
using his Top-Oil Attack Streamliner in
Utah, USA on the 25th of September 2010.
